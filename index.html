Frequently Asked Questions

  • What are the main goals and idea of Fab Lab Hail?

    Spreading digital manufacturing skills and increasing knowledge and creativity to produce an innovative generation capable of manufacturing.

  • What can I make in Fab Lab Hail??

    Almost everything, from shirts to furniture and robots You will learn digital design tools used by experts in the field You will get hands-on training in using equipment for manufacturing

  • Who can use the Fab Lab?

    The FabLab is available to everyone, from students of fifteen years, to university students, small and large companies and research institutes. All of these people can use the FabLab. Our goal is to allow everyone to use the manufacturing equipment to realize their dreams and make them come true.

  • I have an idea and I want Fab Lab to implement it for me, is that possible?

    The Fab Lab Hail team will support you and help you make it yourself

  • Does Fab Lab Hail provide any training?

    The Fab Lab provides workshops, you can review our weekly calendar to learn about the workshops offered
